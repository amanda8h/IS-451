The Seattle Museum of Fashion

Welcome to the Seattle Museum of Fashion (SMoF), where La Mode meets timeless elegance.

SMoF newly open in January 2024. Our museum is dedicated to assembling fashion enthusiasts and art lovers. We commit to creating an accessible artistic environment within the community.

Immerse yourself in a world of avant-garde fashion and explore the boundaries of creativity and self-expression.

Click "Exhibition" to explore the cutting-edge of fashion in the most current event.
